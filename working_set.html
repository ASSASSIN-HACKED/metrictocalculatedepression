THE 101 sight for depressed people

No time to wait till the mouth can enrich the smile the eyes began. Through this site our goal is to check if you have depression or not

Depression is a common mental health problem that causes people to experience low mood, loss of interest or pleasure, feelings of guilt or low self- worth, disturbed sleep or appetite, low energy, and poor concentration. Mental illness is caused due to prolonged depression. It may lead to various hazards like strain, fatigue, abnormal behaviour. In worst scenarios it may even lead to suicide. Due to these reasons we are spreading awareness and helping people to fight it through various analysis based on questions we framed. These would help the victims to recover using techniques like ANNOVA (commonly used method to predict the level of depression). Moderating variables and implications for the prescription of exercise as an effective treatment for depression are discussed.
